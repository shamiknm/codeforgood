About

Code for Good connects UCLA students with local nonprofit organizations to make a positive impact with code.

How does it work?

We connect each member with a nonprofit partner doing work which the member is passionate about. Our members work with the nonprofit to develop a project idea, and execute on that idea over the course of the quarter.

We work closely with both the nonprofits and our members throughout the process, arranging onsite visits and providing space and guidance. At the end of the quarter, members are able to show off what they’ve built to a gathering of mentors and advisors, the UCLA community, and representatives of partner nonprofits.
